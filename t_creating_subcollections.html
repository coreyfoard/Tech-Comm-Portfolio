Creating Subcollections

You can create subcollections within your top-level collection.

You can store items in subcollections just as you would in a top-level collection. Adding an item to a subcollection does not duplicate the item, even if it appears in another collection.

  1. Create a subcollection.
  2. Drag an existing collection onto another collection
  3. Right-click an existing collection and select New Subcollection
  4. You can convert a subcollection into a top-level collection by dragging it out of the collection and dropping it into empty space in the left Zotero pane.
