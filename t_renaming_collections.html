Renaming Collections

You can rename collections in order to best represent the collected items.

You may want to rename a collection as your collection grows in order to better represent the items within it. It is not suggested to rename collections too frequently.

  1. Right-click on the collection.
  2. Select Rename Collection and type in the new name of the collection.
  3. Collections are sorted alphabetically in your library. You can use punctuation marks to sort collections to the top of the list.
