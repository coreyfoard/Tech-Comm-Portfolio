Editing Tags

You can use the Tag Selector to edit and modify tags.

You may want to edit tags when deleting a tag from your library or when merging tags.

Renaming a Tag

You can rename a tag by clicking on the "Rename Tag" in the Tag Selector and typing the new name. By doing this, you change the tag name everywhere in your library.
