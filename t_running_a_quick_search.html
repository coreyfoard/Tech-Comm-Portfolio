Running a Quick Search

Quick searches provide a fast way to find items in a library or collection.

Quick searching is the fastest way to locate an item in your library. When running a quick search, the search will likely return multiple semi-specific results.

  1. Click inside of the search box at the top right of the center pane.
  2. Type search terms.

As you type, only those items in the center column that match the search terms will remain.
