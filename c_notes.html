Notes

Notes can be acced to your library just as other items in your library.

In addition to items and file attachments, you can also store notes in your Zotero library: child notes, which belong to a specific item, and standalone notes. Notes are synced along with item metadata; they don't count against your Zotero file storage quota. Notes are searchable and support HTML formatting.

Child Note

Child notes are notes that belongs to a specific item.

Standalone Note

Standalone notes are notes that are note directly related to any item. Standalone notes will appear alongside any other items in your library.
